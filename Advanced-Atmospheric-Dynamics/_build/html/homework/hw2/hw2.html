Note#

The vector momentum equation as derived with minimum assumptions is

\[\frac{D\mathbf{V}}{Dt} + 2\boldsymbol{\Omega} \times \mathbf{V} = -\frac{1}{\rho} \nabla_{\mathbf{z}} p - \mathbf{g} + \nu \nabla^2 \mathbf{V}\]

The component equations in spherical coordinates (longitude \(\lambda\), latitude \(\phi\) , and vertical distance \(r\)) without the viscous term are

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{Du}{Dt} -& \left( 2\Omega + \frac{u}{r \cos \phi} \right) (v \sin \phi - w \cos \phi) &=& -\frac{1}{\rho r \cos \phi} \frac{\partial p}{\partial \lambda} \tag{1.1} \\ \frac{Dv}{Dt} +& \left( 2\Omega + \frac{u}{r \cos \phi} \right) u \sin \phi + \frac{vw}{r} &=& -\frac{1}{\rho r} \frac{\partial p}{\partial \phi} \tag{1.2} \\ \frac{Dw}{Dt} -& \left( 2\Omega + \frac{u}{r \cos \phi} \right) u \cos \phi - \frac{v^2}{r} &=& -\frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial p}{\partial r} - g, \tag{1.3} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

where the material derivative in \((1)\) can be expressed as

\[\frac{D}{Dt} = \frac{\partial}{\partial t} + \frac{u}{r \cos \phi} \frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda} + \frac{v}{r} \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi} + w \frac{\partial}{\partial r} \]

The above equations are called the Nonhydrostatic Primitive Equations or The Exact Primitive Equations. They are not widely used at present. For large scale numerical weather prediction and general circulation modeling two approximations are made The Traditional Approximation and The Quasi-static Approximation. The Traditional Approximation involves an approximation to the metric expression while The Quasi-static Approximation involves an approximation to the vertical equation. With the ”traditional” approximation \(z \ll a\) ( \(a \sim 6371 \text{km}\) , the earth radius), the above component equations can be written as

\[\frac{Du}{Dt} - \frac{uv \tan \phi}{a} = -\frac{1}{\rho a \cos \phi} \frac{\partial p}{\partial \lambda} + 2\Omega v \sin \phi, \tag{2.1}\]
\[\frac{Dv}{Dt} + \frac{u^2 \tan \phi}{a} = -\frac{1}{\rho a} \frac{\partial p}{\partial \phi} - 2\Omega u \sin \phi, \tag{2.2}\]
\[\frac{Dw}{Dt} = -\frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial p}{\partial z} - g, \tag{2.3}\]

where the material derivative in \((2)\) can be expressed as

\[\frac{D}{Dt} = \frac{\partial}{\partial t} + \frac{u}{a \cos \phi} \frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda} + \frac{v}{a} \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi} + w \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\]

The quasi-static approximation involves the neglect of \(\frac{Dw}{Dt}\) in \((2.3)\). When the \(\frac{Dw}{Dt}\) is omitted in \((2.3)\) , we have the so called “Quasi-static Primitive Equations”. The Quasi-static Primitive Equations are widely used in the present NWP models and general circulation models.

簡單來說:#

1. 向量動量方程式 (Vector Momentum Equation)#

這是基於最少假設推導出的方程式

  • \(\frac{DV}{Dt}+2\Omega\times V=-\frac{1}{\rho}\nabla_{z}p-g+\nu\nabla^{2}V\)

2. 非靜力原始方程式 (Nonhydrostatic Primitive Equations) / 精確原始方程式 (Exact Primitive Equations)#

這是球座標下省略黏滯項 (viscous term) 的分量方程式

  • 分量公式：

    • \(\frac{Du}{Dt}-(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r~cos~\phi})(v~sin~\phi-w~cos~\phi)=-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{r~cos~\phi\partial\lambda}\)

    • \(\frac{Dv}{Dt}+(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r~cos~\phi})u~sin~\phi+\frac{vw}{r}=-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{r\partial\phi}\)

    • \(\frac{Dw}{Dt}-(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r~cos~\phi})u~cos~\phi-\frac{v^{2}}{r}=-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial r}-g\)

  • 對應的全導數 (Material Derivative)：

    • \(\frac{D}{Dt}=\frac{\partial}{\partial t}+\frac{u}{r~cos~\phi}\frac{\partial}{\partial\lambda}+\frac{v}{r}\frac{\partial}{\partial\phi}+w\frac{\partial}{\partial r}\)

3. 傳統近似方程式 (Traditional Approximation Equations)#

引入了 \(z<<a\) (\(a\) 為地球半徑) 的度規表達式近似 (metric expression)

  • 分量公式：

    • \(\frac{Du}{Dt}-\frac{uv~tan~\phi}{a}=-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{a~cos~\phi\partial\lambda}+2\Omega v~sin~\phi\)

    • \(\frac{Dv}{Dt}+\frac{u^{2}tan~\phi}{a}=-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{a\partial\phi}-2\Omega u~sin~\phi\)

    • \(\frac{Dw}{Dt}=-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial z}-g\)

  • 對應的全導數 (Material Derivative)：

    • \(\frac{D}{Dt}=\frac{\partial}{\partial t}+\frac{u}{a~cos~\phi}\frac{\partial}{\partial\lambda}+\frac{v}{a}\frac{\partial}{\partial\phi}+w\frac{\partial}{\partial z}\)

4. 準靜力原始方程式 (Quasi-static Primitive Equations)#

這是針對垂直方程式進行近似的結果，具體作法是在採用「傳統近似」的方程式中忽略垂直方向方程式裡的 \(Dw/Dt\) 項， NWP常用這個😊

  • 分量公式：

    • \(\frac{Du}{Dt}-\frac{uv~tan~\phi}{a}=-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{a~cos~\phi\partial\lambda}+2\Omega v~sin~\phi\)

    • \(\frac{Dv}{Dt}+\frac{u^{2}tan~\phi}{a}=-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{a\partial\phi}-2\Omega u~sin~\phi\)

    • \(0=-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial z}-g\)

  • 對應的全導數 (Material Derivative)：

    • \(\frac{D}{Dt}=\frac{\partial}{\partial t}+\frac{u}{a~cos~\phi}\frac{\partial}{\partial\lambda}+\frac{v}{a}\frac{\partial}{\partial\phi}+w\frac{\partial}{\partial z}\)

Answer 1#

假設與已知 (Assumptions & Preliminaries)#

  • 【已知 1】 球面座標全導數：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{D \lambda }{Dt} &=& \frac{u}{r\cos\phi}\\ \frac{D \phi }{Dt} &=& \frac{v}{r}\\ \frac{D r }{Dt} &=& w\\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 2】 微積分✨1.1：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi] &=& \frac{Du}{Dt} r\cos\phi + u \frac{D r}{Dt} \cos\phi + ur \frac{D \cos\phi}{Dt} \\ \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi]&\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& \frac{Du}{Dt} r\cos\phi + u w \cos\phi - ur \sin\phi \frac{D \phi}{Dt} \\ \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi]&\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& \frac{Du}{Dt} r\cos\phi + u w \cos\phi - ur \sin\phi \frac{v}{r} \\ \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi]&=& \frac{Du}{Dt} r\cos\phi - u(v\sin\phi - w\cos\phi)\\ \frac{Du}{Dt} r\cos\phi &=& \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi] + u(v\sin\phi - w\cos\phi)\\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 3】 微積分✨✨1.1：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega r \cos \phi + u) r \cos \phi] &=& \frac{D}{Dt}[\Omega r^2\cos^2\phi] + \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi] \\ \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega r \cos \phi + u) r \cos \phi] - \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi] &=& \frac{D}{Dt}[\Omega r^2\cos^2\phi] \\ \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega r \cos \phi + u) r \cos \phi] - \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi] &=& \Omega 2r\frac{Dr}{Dt}\cos^2\phi + \Omega r^2 \cdot 2\cos\phi(-\sin\phi)\frac{D\phi}{Dt}\\ \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega r \cos \phi + u) r \cos \phi] - \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi] &\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& \Omega 2rw\cos^2\phi - \Omega r^2 \cdot 2\cos\phi \sin\phi \frac{v}{r}\\ \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega r \cos \phi + u) r \cos \phi] - \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi] &=& 2\Omega r\cos\phi(w\cos\phi - v\sin\phi)\\ \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega r \cos \phi + u) r \cos \phi] - \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi] &=&-2\Omega r\cos\phi(v\sin\phi - w\cos\phi)\\ \frac{D}{Dt}[ur\cos\phi] &=&\frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega r \cos \phi + u) r \cos \phi] +2\Omega r\cos\phi(v\sin\phi - w\cos\phi)\\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 4】 微積分✨1.2：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi] &=& \frac{Du}{Dt} a\cos\phi + u \frac{D a}{Dt} \cos\phi + ua \frac{D \cos\phi}{Dt} \\ \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi]&\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& \frac{Du}{Dt} a\cos\phi + 0 - ua \sin\phi \frac{D \phi}{Dt} \\ \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi]&\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& \frac{Du}{Dt} a\cos\phi - ua \sin\phi \frac{v}{a} \\ \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi]&=& \frac{Du}{Dt} a\cos\phi - uv\sin\phi \\ \frac{Du}{Dt} a\cos\phi &=& \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi] + uv\sin\phi \\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 5】 微積分✨✨1.2：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega a \cos \phi + u) a \cos \phi] &=& \frac{D}{Dt}[\Omega a^2\cos^2\phi] + \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi] \\ \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega a \cos \phi + u) a \cos \phi] - \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi] &=& \frac{D}{Dt}[\Omega a^2\cos^2\phi] \\ \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega a \cos \phi + u) a \cos \phi] - \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi] &=& \Omega 2a\frac{Da}{Dt}\cos^2\phi + \Omega a^2 \cdot 2\cos\phi(-\sin\phi)\frac{D\phi}{Dt}\\ \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega a \cos \phi + u) a \cos \phi] - \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi] &\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& 0- \Omega a^2 \cdot 2\cos\phi \sin\phi \frac{v}{a}\\ \frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega a \cos \phi + u) a \cos \phi] - \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi] &=& -2\Omega av\cos\phi \sin\phi\\ \frac{D}{Dt}[ua\cos\phi] &=&\frac{D}{Dt} [(\Omega a \cos \phi + u) a \cos \phi] +2\Omega av\cos\phi \sin\phi\\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

Q1.3 \((4)\) Is Distorted Form of \((3)\)#

  • 在精確的物理世界 \((3)\)：

    • 角動量的定義中包含了實際到地心的距離 \(r\)。如果一個空氣區塊純粹作垂直上升運動（\(w > 0\)，所以 \(r\) 變大），即便它沒有南北移動（\(v = 0\)），它的絕對旋轉半徑也變大了。為了守恆，它的東向風速（緯向風速 \(u\)）就必須隨之調整減少。

  • 在傳統近似的世界 \((4)\)：

    • 為了簡化計算，公式將原本的距離變數 \(r\) 強制替換成了常數 \(a\) ，也就是假設流體不管怎麼上下運動，它距離地心的距離「永遠一樣」。結果就是：當空氣區塊純粹垂直上升時，因為數學公式中半徑 \(a\) 不變，系統不會強制改變它的東西向風速 \(u\)

Answer 2#

假設與已知 (Assumptions & Preliminaries)#

  • 【已知 1】 總動能 \(KE\)：

    \[K = \frac{1}{2}(u^2+v^2+w^2)\]

  • 【已知 2】\(x\) 方向 \((1.1)\) 式同乘 \(u\) ：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{Du}{Dt} - \left(2\Omega+\frac{1}{r\cos\phi}\right)(v\sin\phi-w\cos\phi) &\overset{\text{(1.1)}}{=}& -\frac{1}{\rho r\cos\phi}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\lambda}\\ u\frac{Du}{Dt} - u\left(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r\cos\phi}\right)(v\sin\phi-w\cos\phi) &=& -\frac{u}{\rho r\cos\phi}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\lambda} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 3】\(y\) 方向 \((1.2)\) 式同乘 \(v\) ：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{Dv}{Dt} + \left(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r\cos\phi}\right)u\sin\phi + \frac{vw}{r} &\overset{\text{(1.2)}}{=}& -\frac{1}{\rho r}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\phi}\\ v\frac{Dv}{Dt} + v\left(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r\cos\phi}\right)u\sin\phi + \frac{v^2w}{r} &=& -\frac{v}{\rho r}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\phi} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 4】\(z\) 方向 \((1.3)\) 式同乘 \(w\) ：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{Dw}{Dt} - \left(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r\cos\phi}\right)u\cos\phi - \frac{v^2}{r} &\overset{\text{(1.3)}}{=}& -\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial r} - g\\ w\frac{Dw}{Dt} - w\left(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r\cos\phi}\right)u\cos\phi - \frac{wv^2}{r} &=& -\frac{w}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial r} - gw \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 5】\(x\) 方向 \((2.1)\) 式同乘 \(u\)：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{Du}{Dt} - \frac{uv\tan\phi}{a} &\overset{\text{(2.1)}}{=}& -\frac{1}{\rho a\cos\phi}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\lambda} + 2\Omega v\sin\phi\\ u\frac{Du}{Dt} - \frac{u^2v\tan\phi}{a} &=& -\frac{u}{\rho a\cos\phi}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\lambda} + 2\Omega uv\sin\phi \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 6】\(y\) 方向 \((2.2)\) 式同乘 \(v\)：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{Dv}{Dt} + \frac{u^2\tan\phi}{a} &\overset{\text{(2.2)}}{=}& -\frac{1}{\rho a}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\phi} - 2\Omega u\sin\phi\\ v\frac{Dv}{Dt} + \frac{vu^2\tan\phi}{a} &=& -\frac{v}{\rho a}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\phi} - 2\Omega uv\sin\phi \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 7】\(z\) 方向 \((2.3)\) 式同乘 \(w\)：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{Dw}{Dt} &\overset{\text{(2.3)}}{=}& -\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial z} - g\\ w\frac{Dw}{Dt} &=& -\frac{w}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial z} - gw \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

Q2.1 \(KE\) of Exact Primitive Equations#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{D KE}{Dt} &\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}&& \frac{D }{Dt}\left[ \frac{1}{2}(u^2+v^2+w^2) \right]\\ &=&& u\frac{Du }{Dt}+ v\frac{D v}{Dt} + w\frac{D w}{Dt}\\ &\overset{\text{已知 2,3,4}}{=}&& -\frac{u}{\rho r\cos\phi}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\lambda} + u\left(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r\cos\phi}\right)(v\sin\phi-w\cos\phi)\\ &&+&-\frac{v}{\rho r}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\phi} - v\left(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r\cos\phi}\right)u\sin\phi - \frac{v^2w}{r}\\ &&+&-\frac{w}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial r} - gw +w\left(2\Omega+\frac{u}{r\cos\phi}\right)u\cos\phi + \frac{wv^2}{r}\\ &=&&-\frac{1}{\rho}\left(\frac{u}{r\cos\phi}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\lambda} + \frac{v}{r}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\phi} + w\frac{\partial p}{\partial r}\right) - gw \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

Q2.2 \(KE\) of Traditional Approximation Equations#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{D KE}{Dt} &\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}&& \frac{D }{Dt}\left[ \frac{1}{2}(u^2+v^2+w^2) \right]\\ &=&& u\frac{Du }{Dt}+ v\frac{D v}{Dt} + w\frac{D w}{Dt}\\ &\overset{\text{已知 5,6,7}}{=}&& -\frac{u}{\rho a\cos\phi}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\lambda} + 2\Omega uv\sin\phi + \frac{u^2v\tan\phi}{a}\\ &&+&-\frac{v}{\rho a}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\phi} - 2\Omega uv\sin\phi - \frac{vu^2\tan\phi}{a}\\ &&+&-\frac{w}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial z} - gw\\ &=&&-\frac{1}{\rho}\left(\frac{u}{a\cos\phi}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\lambda} + \frac{v}{a}\frac{\partial p}{\partial\phi} + w\frac{\partial p}{\partial z}\right) - gw \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 注意到 即使我們做了 「傳統近似」 ，拿掉了某些項並把 \(r\) 換成了 \(a\)，方程式在數學結構上仍然保證了「科氏力與離心力不作功」這個核心的物理與能量守恆原則，傳統近似在NWP 在簡化運算的同時沒有破壞能量守恆 😎🎉

Consider the formation of the subtropical jet in the Hadley circulation .The mean position of the subtropical jet is around \(30^\circ \text{N}\) and typical speed is around \(60 \sim 70 \text{ m s}^{-1}\).

  • 【已知 3】地球上的空氣體積

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} V &\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& \frac{4}{3}\pi(a+h)^3 - \frac{4}{3}\pi a^3\\ &\overset{\text{已知 2}}{=}&\frac{4}{3}\pi (a^3 + 3a^2h + 3ah^2 + h^3 - a^3)\\ &=&\frac{4}{3}\pi \cdot 3a^2h \left(1 + \frac{3ah^2}{3a^2h} + \frac{h^3}{3a^2h}\right)\\ &=&4\pi a^2 h \left(1 + \frac{h}{a} + \frac{1}{3}\frac{h^2}{a^2}\right) \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 3】球面積公式

    \[A = 4\pi a^2\]

Q5.1 proof#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} p &\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& \frac{W}{A} \\ &=&\frac{ M \cdot g }{A}\\ &=&\frac{ \rho \cdot V \cdot g }{A}\\ &\overset{\text{已知 3}}{=}&\frac{ \rho \cdot 4\pi a^2 h \left(1 + \frac{h}{a} + \frac{1}{3}\frac{h^2}{a^2}\right) \cdot g }{A}\\ &\overset{\text{已知 4}}{=}&\frac{ \rho \cdot 4\pi a^2 h \left(1 + \frac{h}{a} + \frac{1}{3}\frac{h^2}{a^2}\right) \cdot g }{4\pi a^2}\\ &=& \rho gh \left(1 + \frac{h}{a} + \frac{1}{3}\frac{h^2}{a^2}\right) \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

Q5.2 discuss the error#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{p_{\text{normal}} - p_{\text{exact}}}{p_{\text{normal}}} &=& \frac{\rho gh - \left(\rho gh + \rho gh\left(\frac{h}{a}\right) + \rho gh\left(\frac{1}{3}\frac{h^2}{a^2}\right)\right)}{\rho gh}\\ &=& - \left(\frac{h}{a}\right) - \left(\frac{1}{3}\frac{h^2}{a^2}\right)\\ &=& - \frac{10}{6371} - \frac{1}{3}(0.00157)^2 \\ &\approx& - 0.00157 - 0.0000008 &\approx& -0.00157 \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

這個誤差幾乎可以忽略

Eigenfunctions and eigenvalues of the fully compressible model.#

We begin with a fully compressible nonhydrostatic model

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{Du}{Dt} &=& -\frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial p}{\partial x}, \tag{1a} \\ \frac{Dv}{Dt} &=& -\frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial p}{\partial y}, \tag{1b} \\ \frac{Dw}{Dt} &=& -\frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial p}{\partial z} - g, \tag{1c} \\ \frac{D\rho}{Dt} &=& -\rho \left( \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial v}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial w}{\partial z} \right), \tag{1d} \\ \frac{D\rho}{Dt} &=& \frac{1}{c^2} \frac{Dp}{Dt}, \tag{1e} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

where \(c^2\) is the square of sound speed and \(D/Dt = \partial/\partial t + u\partial/\partial x + v\partial/\partial y + w\partial/\partial z\).

Let us divide the pressure and density into isothermal basic states and deviation parts, i.e.,

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} P(x, y, z, t) &=& \bar{p}(z) + p'(x, y, z, t), \\ \rho(x, y, z, t) &=& \bar{\rho}(z) + \rho'(x, y, z, t), \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

where \(\partial\bar{p}/\partial z = -\bar{\rho}g\).

With a resting basic state in an isothermal atmosphere (temperature \(T_0\)), (1) can be linearized in the form (with the nonlinear terms on the right hand side)

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left(\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}u\right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \left( \frac{p'}{\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}} \right) &=& N_1, \tag{2a} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left(\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}v\right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \left( \frac{p'}{\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}} \right) &=& N_2, \tag{2b} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left(\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}w\right) + \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma \right) \left( \frac{p'}{\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}} \right) + g \left( \frac{1}{\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}} \left(\rho' - \frac{p'}{c^2}\right) \right) &=& N_3, \tag{2c} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left( \frac{p'}{\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}} \right) + c^2 \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \left(\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}u\right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \left(\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}v\right) + \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial z} - \Gamma \right) \left(\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}w\right) \right) &=& N_4, \tag{2d} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left( \frac{1}{\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}} \left(\rho' - \frac{p'}{c^2}\right) \right) - \frac{N^2}{g} \left(\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}}w\right) &=& N_5, \tag{2e} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

where the constant parameters in the isothermal atmosphere are

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} c^2 &=& \gamma R_d T_0, \tag{3a} \\ \Gamma &=& \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{g}{c^2} - \frac{N^2}{g} \right), \tag{3b} \\ N^2 &=& \frac{\kappa g}{H_s}, \tag{3c} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

and

\[\begin{gather*} H_s &=& R_d T_0 / g. \tag{3d} \end{gather*}\]

The \(\gamma\), \(R_d\), and \(\kappa\) take the usual meaning in the thermodynamics.

Now define the vectors

\[\begin{split}\tilde{u} = \begin{pmatrix} \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} u \\ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} v \\ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} w \\ p' / \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \\ (\rho' - p'/c^2) / \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} U \\ V \\ W \\ P \\ \Pi \end{pmatrix},\end{split}\]

and

\[\tilde{N} = (N_1, N_2, N_3, N_4, N_5)^T,\]

we can write (2) as

\[\frac{\partial \tilde{u}}{\partial t} + L\tilde{u} = \tilde{N}, \tag{4}\]

Where

\[\begin{split}L = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma\right) & g \\ c^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & c^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & c^2 \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} - \Gamma\right) & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -\frac{N^2}{g} & 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}.\end{split}\]

Next we define the inner product of the vectors \(\tilde{f}\) and \(\tilde{g}\) as

\[(\tilde{f}, \tilde{g}) = \frac{1}{L_x L_y L_z} \int_{0}^{L_z} \int_{0}^{L_y} \int_{0}^{L_x} \left( f_1 g_1^* + f_2 g_2^* + f_3 g_3^* + \frac{1}{c^2} f_4 g_4^* + \frac{g^2}{N^2} f_5 g_5^* \right) dxdydz, \tag{5}\]

where the \(f^*\) is the complex conjugate of \(f\). All the components of \(\tilde{f}\) and \(\tilde{g}\) are periodic in \(x \text{ and } y\) and \(f_3 = g_3 = 0\) at \(z = 0, L_z\) (bottom and top). With the inner product (5) we can show that

\[(L\tilde{f}, \tilde{g}) = -(\tilde{f}, L\tilde{g}), \tag{6}\]

i.e., the operator \(L\) is skew-Hermitian. Skew-Hermitian operator with appropriate boundary conditions have three properties that are of extreme importance in physics, both quantum and classical. The three properties are

  • (1) the eigenvalues of a skew-Hermitian operator are imaginary,

  • (2) the eigenfunctions of a skew-Hermitian operator are orthogonal,

  • (3) the eigenfunctions of a skew-Hermitian operator form a complete set.

Finally, we note that (6) is a energy norm, it consists of the kinetic energy \(\bar{\rho}/2(u^2 + v^2 + w^2)\), the elastic energy \(1/(2\bar{\rho})(p'^2/c^2)\) and the thermobaric energy ( the internal energy)

\[I = \frac{\bar{\rho}}{2} \frac{g^2}{N^2} \left( \frac{\theta'}{\bar{\theta}} \right)^2,\]

or with the linearized equation of state, \((\rho - p'/c^2)\bar{\rho}^{-1/2} = -\bar{\rho}^{-1/2}(\theta'/\bar{\theta})\) ( Eckart 1960),

or with the linearized equation of state, \((\rho' - p'/c^2)\bar{\rho}^{-1/2} = -\bar{\rho}^{1/2}(\theta'/\bar{\theta})\) ( Eckart 1960),

\[I = \frac{g^2}{N^2} \frac{\left( \rho' - \frac{p'}{c^2} \right)^2}{2\bar{\rho}},\]

Thus, we can find the normalized eigenvectors \(\tilde{K}_{klmn}\) of L, which satisfy

\[L\tilde{K}_{klmn} = i\nu_{klmn}\tilde{K}_{klmn} \tag{7}\]

where \(\nu_{klmn}\) is real, and \(k, l, m\) can be related to the wavenumbers \(\bar{k}, \bar{l}, \bar{m}\) by

\[\bar{k} = \frac{2\pi k}{L_x}, \quad \bar{l} = \frac{2\pi l}{L_y}, \quad \bar{m} = \frac{2\pi m}{L_z}.\]

The \(\tilde{K}_{klmn}\) form a complete space.

For a given \(k, l, m\), there are six \(n\)’s which must be considered.

(1) The first gives zero frequency and corresponds to steady, horizontally non-divergent flow. This is really the degeneracy of the internal gravity waves with the wave number vector points in the vertical.

(2) The second frequency corresponds to Lamb waves. This solution was first discovered by Laplace (1778-1779) in discussing thermal oscillations in the atmosphere. He effectively stated that there is a mode with equivalent depth equal to the scale height of the atmosphere, and this will satisfy the tidal equations. This mode is now usually called Lamb wave, following a more complete discussion by Lamb (1910). A feature of this mode is that the velocity is everywhere parallel to the earth’s surface, i.e., \(w = 0\). The propagation speed is the speed of sound and has a value of about \(300\text{ m s}^{-1}\). The eigenvectors are found to be

\[\begin{split}\tilde{K}_{klmn} = A_{klmn} \begin{pmatrix} \bar{k} \\ \bar{l} \\ 0 \\ \nu_{klmn} \\ 0 \end{pmatrix} e^{i(\bar{k}x + \bar{l}y)} e^{-\Gamma z}\end{split}\]
\[ . \tag{8a}\]

with the dispersion relationship

\[\nu_{klmn}^2 = c^2 (\bar{k}^2 + \bar{l}^2), \tag{8b}\]

and the normalization factor

\[A_{klmn} = \left( \frac{(\bar{k}^2 + \bar{l}^2)(1 - e^{-2\Gamma L_z})}{\Gamma L_z} \right)^{-\frac{1}{2}}, \tag{8c}\]

(3) The remaining frequencies are the four roots (two acoustic waves and two buoyancy waves) of

\[\frac{\nu_{klmn}^4}{c^2} - \nu_{klmn}^2 \left( \bar{k}^2 + \bar{l}^2 + \bar{m}^2 + \Gamma^2 + \frac{N^2}{c^2} \right) + (\bar{k}^2 + \bar{l}^2) N^2 = 0, \tag{9a}\]

with the eigenvectors

\[\begin{split}\tilde{K}_{klmn} = A_{klmn} \begin{pmatrix} \bar{k} (\bar{m} \cos \bar{m}z - \Gamma \sin \bar{m}z) \\ \bar{l} (\bar{m} \cos \bar{m}z - \Gamma \sin \bar{m}z) \\ -\frac{(\bar{m}^2 + \Gamma^2)\nu_{klmn}^2}{N^2 - \nu_{klmn}^2} i \sin \bar{m}z \\ \nu_{klmn} (\bar{m} \cos \bar{m}z - \Gamma \sin \bar{m}z) \\ \frac{N^2}{g} \frac{(\bar{m}^2 + \Gamma^2)}{N^2 - \nu_{klmn}^2} \nu_{klmn} \sin \bar{m}z \end{pmatrix} e^{i(\bar{k}x + \bar{l}y)}\end{split}\]
\[ . \tag{9b}\]

Where

\[A_{klmn} = \left( \left( \frac{\bar{m}^2 + \Gamma^2}{N^2 - \nu_{klmn}^2} \right) \left( N^2 (\bar{k}^2 + \bar{l}^2) - \frac{\nu_{klmn}^4}{c^2} \right) \right)^{-\frac{1}{2}}. \tag{9c}\]

If we take the inner product of (4) with \(\tilde{K}_{klmn}\) to obtain

\[\left( \frac{\partial \tilde{u}}{\partial t}, \tilde{K}_{klmn} \right) + (L\tilde{u}, \tilde{K}_{klmn}) = (\tilde{N}, \tilde{K}_{klmn}). \tag{10}\]

With the skew-Hermitian property, we can write the second term of (10) as

\[(L\tilde{u}, \tilde{K}_{klmn}) = -(\tilde{u}, L\tilde{K}_{klmn}) = i\nu_{klmn} (\tilde{u}, \tilde{K}_{klmn}). \tag{11}\]

Now define the transform pairs of

\[\hat{u}_{klmn}(t) = (\tilde{u}, \tilde{K}_{klmn}), \tag{12a}\]

with inverse

\[\tilde{u} = \sum_{klmn} \hat{u}_{klmn}(t) \tilde{K}_{klmn}(x, y, z), \tag{12b}\]

\(\tilde{N}\) and \(\hat{N}_{klmn}\) can be defined similarly.

We can use (12) to write (10) as a set of scalar equations

\[\frac{d\hat{u}_{klmn}(t)}{dt} + i\nu_{klmn}\hat{u}_{klmn}(t) = \hat{N}_{klmn}, \tag{13}\]

which are the desired spectral equations. Equation (13) can be easily integrated as

\[\hat{u}_{klmn}(t + \Delta t) = \hat{u}_{klmn}(t)e^{-i\nu_{klmn}(\Delta t)} + \int_{t}^{t+\Delta t} \hat{N}_{klmn} e^{i\nu_{klmn}(t' - t - \Delta t)} dt'. \tag{14}\]

The computational procedures are:

  • (1) Predict \(\hat{u}_{klmn}\) in spectral space.

  • (2) Transform \(\hat{u}_{klmn}\) to physical space to get \(\tilde{u}\).

  • (3) Evaluate \(\tilde{N}\) in physical space.

  • (4) Transform \(\tilde{N}\) to spectral space to get \(\hat{N}_{klmn}\).

  • (5) Go to step (1).

In step (3) and (4), the transform method of Orszag (1970) can be used. If \(k\) is the truncated wave number, it is then possible to evaluate the \(\hat{N}_{klmn}\) exactly using \(3k + 1\) points trapezoidal quadrature, which prevents aliasing error in the calculation of the transformation of the nonlinear term. Note that the treatment of linear term in (14) is exact, which enlarge the time step of integration without the CFL constraint of the fast linear waves.

一、原始的完全可壓縮流體力學偏微分方程組 轉成 密度加權的線性化微擾方程組（且分離出非線性強迫項） (From the original fully compressible Navier-Stokes equations to the density-weighted linearized perturbation equations (with isolated nonlinear forcing terms))#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{\partial }{\partial t } \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} u \right] + \frac{\partial }{\partial x} \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} p' \right] &=& \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \left(- (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) u + \frac{\rho'}{\bar{\rho} \rho } \frac{\partial p'}{\partial x} \right) \tag{2a}\\ \frac{\partial }{\partial t } \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} v \right] + \frac{\partial }{\partial y} \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} p' \right] &=& \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \left(- (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) v + \frac{\rho'}{\bar{\rho} \rho }\frac{\partial p'}{\partial y} \right) \tag{2b}\\ \frac{\partial }{\partial t } \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} w \right] + g\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}}\left(\rho' - \frac{p'}{c^2}\right) + \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma \right)[\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}}p'] &=& - \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \left( (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) w + \frac{\rho'^2}{\rho \bar{\rho} } g + \frac{\rho'}{\rho \bar{\rho}} \frac{\partial p'}{\partial z} \right) \tag{2c}\\ \frac{\partial ( \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} p') }{\partial t} + c^2 \left( \frac{\partial ( \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} u) }{\partial x } + \frac{\partial (\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} v)}{\partial y } + \left( \frac{\partial }{\partial z} - \Gamma \right)\left[\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} w \right] \right) &=& -\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} \left( (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla ) p' + c^2 \rho' \nabla \cdot \mathbf{u} \right) \tag{2d}\\ \frac{\partial }{\partial t} \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} \left( \rho' - \frac{p'}{c^2} \right) \right] - w \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \frac{N^2}{g} &=& -\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} \left( \mathbf{u}\cdot\nabla \rho' -\frac{1}{c^2} \mathbf{u}\cdot\nabla p' \right) \tag{2e} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • \(\rho \) : 瞬時總密度場 (Instantaneous total density field) \([\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^{-3}]\)

  • \(\bar{\rho }\) : 基本背景態密度 (Basic state density) \([\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^{-3}]\)

  • \(\rho '\) : 密度的時空微擾項 (Density perturbation variable) \([\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^{-3}]\)

  • \(p'\) : 氣壓的時空微擾項 (Pressure perturbation variable) \([\text{Pa}]\)

  • \(\mathbf{u}\): 三維速度向量 \((u, v, w)\) \([\text{m} \cdot \text{s}^{-1}]\)

  • \(g\) : 地表重力加速度常數 (Standard acceleration of gravity) \(g \approx 9.80665 \text{ m}\cdot\text{s}^{-2}\)

  • \(\Gamma \) : 大氣層結修正因子 (Stratification correction factor) \([\text{m}^{-1}]\)

  • \(N^{2}\) : 浮力頻率平方 (Brunt-Väisälä frequency squared) \([\text{s}^{-2}]\)

  • \(c\) : 本地音速 (Local speed of sound) \([\text{m}\cdot\text{s}^{-1}]\)

為什麼要花這麼大功夫轉成這樣？（The Ultimate Goal）#

當方程式轉成具有「反厄米特特性（Skew-Hermitian）」的線性算子 \(L\) 後，數學上會保證三件極度美好的事情（正如講義提到的）：

  1. 特徵值純虛數：這代表方程式的解是純粹的「震盪波」，不會有數值上無端呈指數爆炸的解（保證穩定性）。

  2. 特徵函數正交：你可以用這組解當作基底（Basis），像傅立葉轉換一樣，把大氣真實的狀態拆解成無數個獨立波的疊加。

  3. 精確處理快波：在數值模式（如天氣預報）中，聲波跑得非常快，會嚴重限制時間步長（CFL condition）。把線性項抽出來並精確解析後，程式在時間積分時就可以跨大步長，只針對非線性強迫項 \(N\) 做數值積分（例如講義 (14) 式的處理方式），大幅節省超級電腦的運算資源。

假設與已知 (Assumptions & Preliminaries)#

  • 【假設 1】 忽略黏滯力（Inviscid fluid assumption） ：

    \[ \mu = 0\]

    • \(\mu \) : 動力黏滯係數 (Dynamic viscosity) \([\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^{-1}\cdot\text{s}^{-1}]\)

  • 【已知 1】 動量方程式 (Momentum Equation) - 納維-斯托克斯 (Navier-Stokes) ：

    • (a)

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t } + (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) \mathbf{u} &=& \mathbf{g} - \frac{1}{\rho} \nabla p + \frac{\mu}{\rho} \nabla^2 \mathbf{u} \\ \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t } + (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) \mathbf{u} &\overset{\text{假設 1}}{=}& \mathbf{g} - \frac{1}{\rho} \nabla p + \frac{0}{\rho} \nabla^2 \mathbf{u} \\ \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t } + (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) \mathbf{u} &=& \mathbf{g} - \frac{1}{\rho} \nabla p \\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • (b) x 方向

    \[\frac{\partial u}{\partial t } + (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) u \overset{\text{已知 1(a)}}{=} - \frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial p}{\partial x} \]

    • (c) y 方向

    \[\frac{\partial v}{\partial t } + (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) v \overset{\text{已知 1(a)}}{=} - \frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial p}{\partial y} \]

    • (d) z 方向

    \[\frac{\partial w}{\partial t } + (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) w \overset{\text{已知 1(a)}}{=} -g - \frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial p}{\partial z} \]

    • \(\mathbf{u}\): 三維速度向量 \((u, v, w)\) \([\text{m} \cdot \text{s}^{-1}]\)

    • \(\mathbf{g}\): 重力加速度向量 \((0, 0, -g)\) \([\text{m} \cdot \text{s}^{-2}]\)

    • \(p\): 氣壓 \( [\text{Pa}],[\text{kg} \cdot \text{m}^{-1}\cdot \text{s}^{-2}] \)

    • \(\rho\): 流體密度 \([\text{kg} \cdot \text{m}^{-3}]\)

    • \(\mu\): 動力黏滯係數 (Dynamic viscosity) \([\text{kg} \cdot \text{m}^{-1}\cdot \text{s}^{-1}] \)

  • 【已知 2】 連續方程式 (Continuity Equation) ：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t } + \nabla \cdot (\rho \mathbf{u}) &=& 0 \\ \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t } + \frac{\partial (\rho u)}{\partial x }+ \frac{\partial (\rho v)}{\partial y }+ \frac{\partial (\rho w)}{\partial z } &=& 0 \\ \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t } + \left( \frac{\partial \rho }{\partial x } u + \rho \frac{\partial u}{\partial x } \right)+ \left( \frac{\partial \rho }{\partial y } v + \rho \frac{\partial v}{\partial y } \right) + \left( \frac{\partial \rho }{\partial z } w + \rho \frac{\partial w}{\partial z } \right)&=& 0 \\ \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t } + u \frac{\partial \rho }{\partial x } + v\frac{\partial \rho }{\partial y } + w \frac{\partial \rho }{\partial z } &=& - \rho \left( \frac{\partial u}{\partial x } + \frac{\partial v}{\partial y } +\frac{\partial w}{\partial z }\right)\\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • \(\rho\): 流體密度 \([\text{kg} \cdot \text{m}^{-3}]\)

    • \(\mathbf{u}\): 三維速度向量 \((u, v, w)\) \([\text{m} \cdot \text{s}^{-1}]\)

    • \(t\): 時間 \([\text{s}]\)。

  • 【假設 2】 等熵過程假設（Isentropic process assumption）：

    \[\frac{Ds}{Dt} = 0\]

    • \(s\) : 比熵／單位質量熵 (Specific entropy) \([\text{J}\cdot\text{kg}^{-1}\cdot\text{K}^{-1}]\)

  • 【假設 3】 線性微擾狀態假設（Linear perturbation state assumption） ： 將流體的氣壓與密度分為隨高度變化的基本背景態（Basic state）與隨時空變化的微小擾動態（Perturbation）。

    • (a) 氣壓場拆解：

    \[p(x,y,z,t) = \bar{p}(z) + p'(x,y,z,t)\]

    • (b) 密度場拆解：

    \[\rho(x,y,z,t) = \bar{\rho}(z) + \rho'(x,y,z,t)\]

\Gamma \right)\left[\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} w \right] \right) &=& -\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla ) p' - c^2 \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} \rho' \nabla \cdot \mathbf{u} \\ \frac{\partial ( \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} p') }{\partial t} + c^2 \left( \frac{\partial ( \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} u) }{\partial x } + \frac{\partial (\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} v)}{\partial y } + \left( \frac{\partial }{\partial z} - 假設與已知 (Assumptions & Preliminaries)#

  • 【已知 1】 密度加權的線性化微擾方程組與非線性強迫項分離（Density-weighted linearized perturbation equations with nonlinear forcing separation） ：

    • (a) \(x\) 方向動量方程式：

    \[\frac{\partial }{\partial t } \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} u \right] + \frac{\partial }{\partial x} \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} p' \right] = \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \left(- (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) u + \frac{\rho'}{\bar{\rho} \rho } \frac{\partial p'}{\partial x} \right) \]

    • (b) \(y\) 方向動量方程式：

    \[\frac{\partial }{\partial t } \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} v \right] + \frac{\partial }{\partial y} \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} p' \right] = \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \left(- (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) v + \frac{\rho'}{\bar{\rho} \rho }\frac{\partial p'}{\partial y} \right) \]

    • (c) \(z\) 方向動量方程式：

    \[\frac{\partial }{\partial t } \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} w \right] + g\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}}\left(\rho' - \frac{p'}{c^2}\right) + \left( \frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma \right)[\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}}p'] = - \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \left( (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) w + \frac{\rho'^2}{\rho \bar{\rho} } g + \frac{\rho'}{\rho \bar{\rho}} \frac{\partial p'}{\partial z} \right)\]

    • (d) 結合狀態方程之連續（質量守恆）方程式：

    \[\frac{\partial ( \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} p') }{\partial t} + c^2 \left( \frac{\partial ( \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} u) }{\partial x } + \frac{\partial (\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} v)}{\partial y } + \left( \frac{\partial }{\partial z} - \Gamma \right)\left[\bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} w \right] \right) = -\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} \left( (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla ) p' + c^2 \rho' \nabla \cdot \mathbf{u} \right) \]

    • (e) 內能／熱力學能量（等熵）方程式：

    \[\frac{\partial }{\partial t} \left[ \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} \left( \rho' - \frac{p'}{c^2} \right) \right] - w \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \frac{N^2}{g} = -\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} \left( \mathbf{u}\cdot\nabla \rho' -\frac{1}{c^2} \mathbf{u}\cdot\nabla p' \right)\]

    • \(\rho \) : 瞬時總密度場 (Instantaneous total density field) \([\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^{-3}]\)

    • \(\bar{\rho }\) : 基本背景態密度 (Basic state density) \([\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^{-3}]\)

    • \(\rho '\) : 密度的時空微擾項 (Density perturbation variable) \([\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^{-3}]\)

    • \(p'\) : 氣壓的時空微擾項 (Pressure perturbation variable) \([\text{Pa}]\)

    • \(\mathbf{u}\): 三維速度向量 \((u, v, w)\) \([\text{m} \cdot \text{s}^{-1}]\)

    • \(g\) : 地表重力加速度常數 (Standard acceleration of gravity) \(g \approx 9.80665 \text{ m}\cdot\text{s}^{-2}\)

    • \(\Gamma \) : 大氣層結修正因子 (Stratification correction factor) \([\text{m}^{-1}]\)

    • \(N^{2}\) : 浮力頻率平方 (Brunt-Väisälä frequency squared) \([\text{s}^{-2}]\)

    • \(c\) : 本地音速 (Local speed of sound) \([\text{m}\cdot\text{s}^{-1}]\)

  • 【定義 1】 經密度加權的大氣狀態向量（Density-weighted atmospheric state vector） ：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \tilde{u} &\overset{\text{def}}{=}& \begin{bmatrix} U \\[1.5ex] V \\[1.5ex] W \\[1.5ex] P \\[1.5ex] \Pi \\[1.5ex] \end{bmatrix} &\overset{\text{let}}{=}& \begin{bmatrix} \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} u \\[1.5ex] \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} v \\[1.5ex] \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} w \\[1.5ex] \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} p' \\[1.5ex] \bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} \left( \rho' - \frac{p'}{c^2} \right)\\[1.5ex] \end{bmatrix} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • \(\~{\mathbf{u}}\) : 大氣波動狀態向量 (Atmospheric wave state vector)

    • \(U, V, W\) : 經密度平方根加權後的 \(x, y, z\) 三軸方向動量型速度分量 \([\text{kg}^{\frac{1}{2}}\cdot\text{m}^{-1}\cdot\text{s}^{-1}]\)

    • \(P\) : 經密度倒數平方根加權後的縮放氣壓微擾項 \([\text{kg}^{-\frac{1}{2}}\cdot\text{m}^{\frac{1}{2}}\cdot\text{s}^{-2}]\)

    • \(\Pi \) : 經密度倒數平方根加權後的非等熵度量／勢度微擾項 (Non-isentropic perturbation representative) \([\text{kg}^{\frac{1}{2}}\cdot\text{m}^{-3}]\)

    • \(u, v, w, p', \rho'\) : 原始微擾場的速度、氣壓與密度分量

    • \(\bar{\rho }\) : 基本背景態密度 \([\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^{-3}]\)

    • \(c\) : 本地音速 \([\text{m}\cdot\text{s}^{-1}]\)

  • 【定義 2】 非線性強迫力向量（Nonlinear forcing vector） ：

    \[\begin{split}\tilde{N} \overset{\text{def}}{=} \begin{bmatrix} \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \left(- (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) u + \frac{\rho'}{\bar{\rho} \rho } \frac{\partial p'}{\partial x} \right) \\[2ex] \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \left(- (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) v + \frac{\rho'}{\bar{\rho} \rho }\frac{\partial p'}{\partial y} \right) \\[2ex] - \bar{\rho}^{\frac{1}{2}} \left( (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla) w + \frac{\rho'^2}{\rho \bar{\rho} } g + \frac{\rho'}{\rho \bar{\rho}} \frac{\partial p'}{\partial z} \right) \\[2ex] -\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} \left( (\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla ) p' + c^2 \rho' \nabla \cdot \mathbf{u} \right) \\[2ex] -\bar{\rho}^{\frac{-1}{2}} \left( \mathbf{u}\cdot\nabla \rho' -\frac{1}{c^2} \mathbf{u}\cdot\nabla p' \right) \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

    • \(\tilde{\mathbf{N}}\) : 包含平流與微擾乘積項的五維非線性強迫力向量 (Nonlinear forcing column vector)

    • \(g\) : 地表重力加速度常數 \(g \approx 9.80665 \text{ m}\cdot\text{s}^{-2}\)

    • \(\rho \) : 瞬時總密度場 \([\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^{-3}]\)

    • \(c\) : 本地音速 (Local speed of sound) \([\text{m}\cdot\text{s}^{-1}]\)

    • \((\mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla)\) : 三維平流算符 \([\text{s}^{-1}]\)

  • 【定義 3】 空間微分波動算符矩陣（Spatial differential wave operator matrix） ：

    \[\begin{split} \mathbf{L} \overset{\text{def}}{=} \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & 0 & \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma\right) & g \\[2ex] c^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & c^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & c^2 \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} - \Gamma\right) & 0 & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & -\frac{N^2}{g} & 0 & 0 \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

    • \(\mathbf{L}\) : 封裝空間偏微分與大氣層結常數的 \(5 \times 5\) 矩陣微分算符 (Spatial differential operator matrix)

    • \(\Gamma \) : 大氣層結修正因子 \([\text{m}^{-1}]\)

    • \(N^{2}\) : 浮力頻率平方 \([\text{s}^{-2}]\)

    • \(g\) : 地表重力加速度常數 \(g \approx 9.80665 \text{ m}\cdot\text{s}^{-2}\)

    • \(c\) : 本地音速 (Local speed of sound) \([\text{m}\cdot\text{s}^{-1}]\)

    • \(\frac{\partial}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\) : 各時空空間軸向的偏微分算符 \([\text{m}^{-1}]\)

三、確認算子 \(\mathbf{L}\) 的反厄米特特性#

\[(\mathbf{L}\tilde{f}, \tilde{g}) = -(\tilde{f}, L\tilde{g})\]

算子 \(\mathbf{L}\) 在指定內積 \((\cdot , \cdot)\) 下滿足反厄米特（Anti-Hermitian）特性，意即 \((\mathbf{L}\tilde{f}, \tilde{g}) = -(\tilde{f}, \mathbf{L}\tilde{g})\)。這在物理上意味著該系統不存在能量的憑空耗散或產生，是波動得以穩定傳播的數學保證。

假設與已知 (Assumptions & Preliminaries)#

  • 【已知 1】 空間微分波動算符矩陣（Spatial differential wave operator matrix） ：

    \[\begin{split} \mathbf{L} \overset{\text{def}}{=} \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & 0 & \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma\right) & g \\[2ex] c^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & c^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & c^2 \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} - \Gamma\right) & 0 & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & -\frac{N^2}{g} & 0 & 0 \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

    • \(\mathbf{L}\) : 封裝空間偏微分與大氣層結常數的 \(5 \times 5\) 矩陣微分算符 (Spatial differential operator matrix)

    • \(\Gamma \) : 大氣層結修正因子 \([\text{m}^{-1}]\)

    • \(N^{2}\) : 浮力頻率平方 \([\text{s}^{-2}]\)

    • \(g\) : 地表重力加速度常數 \(g \approx 9.80665 \text{ m}\cdot\text{s}^{-2}\)

    • \(c\) : 本地音速 (Local speed of sound) \([\text{m}\cdot\text{s}^{-1}]\)

    • \(\frac{\partial}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\) : 各時空空間軸向的偏微分算符 \([\text{m}^{-1}]\)

  • 【定義 1】 測試函數向量與大氣波動邊界條件（Test function vectors and atmospheric wave boundary conditions） ：

    • (a) 五維測試函數向量定義：

    \[\begin{split} \tilde{f} \overset{\text{let}}{=} \begin{bmatrix} f_1 \\[2ex] f_2 \\[2ex] f_3 \\[2ex] f_4 \\[2ex] f_5 \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \quad , \quad \tilde{g} \overset{\text{let}}{=} \begin{bmatrix} g_1 \\[2ex] g_2 \\[2ex] g_3 \\[2ex] g_4 \\[2ex] g_5 \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \end{split}\]

    • (b) 水平週期性與垂直剛性壁面邊界條件：

    \[\begin{split}\left\{\begin{matrix} f_i(0, y, z, t) &=& f_i(L_x, y, z, t) &\quad , \quad i \in \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\} \\ f_i(x, 0, z, t) &=& f_i(x, L_y, z, t) &\quad , \quad i \in \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\} \\ f_3(x, y, 0, t) &=& 0 &\quad (\text{底部 } z=0) \\ f_3(x, y, L_z, t) &=& 0 &\quad (\text{頂部 } z=L_z) \\ \end{matrix}\right.\end{split}\]
    \[\begin{split}\left\{\begin{matrix} g_i(0, y, z, t) &=& g_i(L_x, y, z, t) &\quad , \quad i \in \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\} \\ g_i(x, 0, z, t) &=& g_i(x, L_y, z, t) &\quad , \quad i \in \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\} \\ g_3(x, y, 0, t) &=& 0 &\quad (\text{底部 } z=0) \\ g_3(x, y, L_z, t) &=& 0 &\quad (\text{頂部 } z=L_z) \\ \end{matrix}\right.\end{split}\]

    • \(f_i, g_i\) : 向量中的各個純量分量元素 (Vector component elements)，其物理量綱與【定義 1 (前章)】的大氣狀態向量各元件一一對應

    • \(x, y, z\) : 三維空間位置座標 \([\text{m}]\)

    • \(t\) : 時間 \([\text{s}]\)

    • \(L_{x}\) : 系統在 \(x\) 軸方向（東西向）的週期長度 (Domain length in x-direction) \([\text{m}]\)

    • \(L_{y}\) : 系統在 \(y\) 軸方向（南北向）的週期長度 (Domain length in y-direction) \([\text{m}]\)

    • \(L_{z}\) : 大氣層垂直方向的總厚度／頂部高度 (Domain height in z-direction) \([\text{m}]\)

    • \(f_3, g_3\) : 對應垂直速度 \(w\) 的函數分量；在頂部與底部為零（即滿足剛性無穿透條件 Rigid non-penetration boundary condition）

  • 【定義 2】 能量權重空間平均內積（Energy-weighted spatially averaged inner product） ：

    \[ (\tilde{f}, \tilde{g}) \overset{\text{def}}{=} \frac{1}{L_x L_y L_z} \int_{0}^{L_z} \int_{0}^{L_y} \int_{0}^{L_x} \left( f_1 g_1^* + f_2 g_2^* + f_3 g_3^* + \frac{1}{c^2} f_4 g_4^* + \frac{g^2}{N^2} f_5 g_5^* \right) dxdydz \]

    • \((\mathbf{\tilde{f}}, \mathbf{\tilde{g}})\) : 兩個波動狀態向量在全域空間上的內積值 (Spatially averaged inner product) [多元複合單位]

    • \(f_{i}\) : 第一個測試函數向量 \(\bar{\mathbf{f}}\) 的第 \(i\) 個純量分量

    • \(g_{i}^{*}\) : 第二個測試函數向量 \(\bar{\mathbf{g}}\) 的第 \(i\) 個純量分量的共軛複數 (Complex conjugate)

    • \(x, y, z\) : 三維直角空間座標 \([\text{m}]\)\(L_x, L_y\) : 水平方向的長度與寬度 \([\text{m}]\)

    • \(L_{z}\) : 垂直方向的大氣總高度 \([\text{m}]\)

    • \(\frac{1}{L_{x}L_{y}L_{z}}\) : 空間體積歸一化因子 (Volume normalization factor) \([\text{m}^{-3}]\)

    • \(c^{2}\) : 本地音速平方 (Speed of sound squared) \([\text{m}^2\cdot\text{s}^{-2}]\)

    • \(g\) : 地表重力加速度常數 \(g \approx 9.80665 \text{ m}\cdot\text{s}^{-2}\)

    • \(N^{2}\) : 浮力頻率平方 / 班特-外薩拉頻率平方 (Brunt-Väisälä frequency squared) \([\text{s}^{-2}]\)

    • \(\frac{1}{c^2} f_4 g_4^*\) : 經彈性音速權重調整後的彈性可壓縮位能項 (Compressible potential energy component)

    • \(\frac{g^2}{N^2} f_5 g_5^*\) : 經靜力穩定度權重調整後的重力浮力熱力位能項 (Gravitational potential energy component)

  • 【推導 1】 算符矩陣作用於狀態向量的各分量展開（Component expansion of operator matrix acting on state vectors） ：

    • (a) 算符矩陣 \(\mathbf{L}\) 作用於測試向量 \(\bar{\mathbf{f}}\)：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \mathbf{L}\tilde{f} &\overset{\text{已知 1,定義 1(a)}}{=}& \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & 0 & \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma\right) & g \\[2ex] c^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & c^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & c^2 \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} - \Gamma\right) & 0 & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & -\frac{N^2}{g} & 0 & 0 \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} f_1 \\[2ex] f_2 \\[2ex] f_3 \\[2ex] f_4 \\[2ex] f_5 \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \\ &=& \begin{bmatrix} \frac{\partial f_4}{\partial x} \\[2ex] \frac{\partial f_4}{\partial y} \\[2ex] \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma\right)f_4 + g f_5 \\[2ex] c^2 \left[ \frac{\partial f_1}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial f_2}{\partial y} + \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} - \Gamma\right)f_3 \right] \\[2ex] -\frac{N^2}{g} f_3 \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • (b) 算符矩陣 \(\mathbf{L}\) 作用於測試向量 \(\bar{\mathbf{g}}\)：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \mathbf{L}\tilde{g} &\overset{\text{已知 1,定義 1(a)}}{=}& \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & 0 & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & 0 & \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma\right) & g \\[2ex] c^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & c^2 \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & c^2 \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} - \Gamma\right) & 0 & 0 \\[2ex] 0 & 0 & -\frac{N^2}{g} & 0 & 0 \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} g_1 \\[2ex] g_2 \\[2ex] g_3 \\[2ex] g_4 \\[2ex] g_5 \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \\ &=& \begin{bmatrix} \frac{\partial g_4}{\partial x} \\[2ex] \frac{\partial g_4}{\partial y} \\[2ex] \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma\right)g_4 + g g_5 \\[2ex] c^2 \left[ \frac{\partial g_1}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial g_2}{\partial y} + \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} - \Gamma\right)g_3 \right] \\[2ex] -\frac{N^2}{g} g_3 \\[2ex] \end{bmatrix} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • \(\mathbf{L}\) : 空間微分波動算符矩陣

    • \(\mathbf{\tilde{f}}, \mathbf{\tilde{g}}\) : 五維測試函數向量\(f_i , g_i\) : 各向量對應之純量分量元素

    • \(\Gamma \) : 大氣層結修正因子 \([\text{m}^{-1}]\)

    • \(N^{2}\) : 浮力頻率平方 \([\text{s}^{-2}]\)

    • \(c^{2}\) : 本地音速平方 \([\text{m}^2\cdot\text{s}^{-2}]\)

    • \(g\) : 地表重力加速度常數 \(g \approx 9.80665 \text{ m}\cdot\text{s}^{-2}\)

  • 【推導 2】 單維度分部積分與邊界項消去（Single-dimensional integration by parts and boundary term elimination） ：

    • (a) \(x\) 方向之 \(f_{4}\) 微分項分部積分：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \int_{0}^{L_x} \frac{\partial f_4}{\partial x}g_1^* \ dx &\overset{\text{IbP}}{=}& \left[ f_4 \cdot g_1^* \right]_{0}^{L_x} - \int_{0}^{L_x} \frac{\partial g_1^*}{\partial x} f_4 \ dx \\ &=& \left( f_4(L_x) \cdot g_1^*(L_x) - f_4(0) \cdot g_1^*(0) \right) - \int_{0}^{L_x} \frac{\partial g_1^*}{\partial x} f_4 \ dx \\ &\overset{\text{定義 1(b)}}{=}& \left( f_4(0) \cdot g_1^*(0) - f_4(0) \cdot g_1^*(0) \right) - \int_{0}^{L_x} \frac{\partial g_1^*}{\partial x} f_4 \ dx \\ &=& - \int_{0}^{L_x} \frac{\partial g_1^*}{\partial x} f_4 \ dx \\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • (b) \(y\) 方向之 \(f_{4}\) 微分項分部積分：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \int_{0}^{L_y} \frac{\partial f_4}{\partial y}g_2^* \ dy &\overset{\text{IbP}}{=}& \left[ f_4 \cdot g_2^* \right]_{0}^{L_y} - \int_{0}^{L_y} \frac{\partial g_2^*}{\partial y} f_4 \ dy \\ &=& \left( f_4(L_y) \cdot g_2^*(L_y) - f_4(0) \cdot g_2^*(0) \right) - \int_{0}^{L_y} \frac{\partial g_2^*}{\partial y} f_4 \ dy \\ &\overset{\text{定義 1(b)}}{=}& \left( f_4(0) \cdot g_2^*(0) - f_4(0) \cdot g_2^*(0) \right) - \int_{0}^{L_y} \frac{\partial g_2^*}{\partial y} f_4 \ dy \\ &=& - \int_{0}^{L_y} \frac{\partial g_2^*}{\partial y} f_4 \ dy \\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • (c) \(z\) 方向之 \(f_{4}\) 微分項分部積分：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \int_{0}^{L_z} \frac{\partial f_4}{\partial z}g_3^* \ dz &\overset{\text{IbP}}{=}& \left[ f_4 \cdot g_3^* \right]_{0}^{L_z} - \int_{0}^{L_z} \frac{\partial g_3^*}{\partial z} f_4 \ dz \\ &=& \left( f_4(L_z) \cdot g_3^*(L_z) - f_4(0) \cdot g_3^*(0) \right) - \int_{0}^{L_z} \frac{\partial g_3^*}{\partial z} f_4 \ dz \\ &\overset{\text{定義 1(b)}}{=}& \left( 0 \cdot 0 - 0 \cdot 0 \right) - \int_{0}^{L_z} \frac{\partial g_3^*}{\partial z} f_4 \ dz \\ &=& - \int_{0}^{L_z} \frac{\partial g_3^*}{\partial z} f_4 \ dz \\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • (d) \(x\) 方向之 \(f_{1}\) 微分項分部積分：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \int_{0}^{L_x} \frac{\partial f_1}{\partial x}g_4^* \ dx &\overset{\text{IbP}}{=}& \left[ f_1 \cdot g_4^* \right]_{0}^{L_x} - \int_{0}^{L_x} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial x} f_1 \ dx \\ &=& \left( f_1(L_x) \cdot g_4^*(L_x) - f_1(0) \cdot g_4^*(0) \right) - \int_{0}^{L_x} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial x} f_1 \ dx \\ &\overset{\text{定義 1(b)}}{=}& \left( f_1(0) \cdot g_4^*(0) - f_1(0) \cdot g_4^*(0) \right) - \int_{0}^{L_x} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial x} f_1 \ dx \\ &=& - \int_{0}^{L_x} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial x} f_1 \ dx \\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • (e) \(y\) 方向之 \(f_{2}\) 微分項分部積分：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \int_{0}^{L_y} \frac{\partial f_2}{\partial y}g_4^* \ dy &\overset{\text{IbP}}{=}& \left[ f_2 \cdot g_4^* \right]_{0}^{L_y} - \int_{0}^{L_y} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial y} f_2 \ dy \\ &=& \left( f_2(L_y) \cdot g_4^*(L_y) - f_2(0) \cdot g_4^*(0) \right) - \int_{0}^{L_y} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial y} f_2 \ dy \\ &\overset{\text{定義 1(b)}}{=}& \left( f_2(0) \cdot g_4^*(0) - f_2(0) \cdot g_4^*(0) \right) - \int_{0}^{L_y} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial y} f_2 \ dy \\ &=& - \int_{0}^{L_y} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial y} f_2 \ dy \\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • (f) \(z\) 方向之 \(f_{3}\) 微分項分部積分：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \int_{0}^{L_z} \frac{\partial f_3}{\partial z}g_4^* \ dz &\overset{\text{IbP}}{=}& \left[ f_3 \cdot g_4^* \right]_{0}^{L_z} - \int_{0}^{L_z} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial z} f_3 \ dz \\ &=& \left( f_3(L_z) \cdot g_4^*(L_z) - f_3(0) \cdot g_4^*(0) \right) - \int_{0}^{L_z} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial z} f_3 \ dz \\ &\overset{\text{定義 1(b)}}{=}& \left( 0 \cdot 0 - 0 \cdot 0 \right) - \int_{0}^{L_z} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial z} f_3 \ dz \\ &=& - \int_{0}^{L_z} \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial z} f_3 \ dz \\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • \(\text{IbP}\) : 分部積分法 (Integration by Parts) 算符法則

    • \(f_i, g_i\) : 測試函數向量的空間連續分量元素

    • \(g_{i}^{*}\) : 測試函數分量的共軛複數 (Complex conjugate)

    • \(x, y, z\) : 水平與垂直空間位置座標 \([\text{m}]\)

    • \(L_x, L_y\) : 水平邊界邊長週期特徵長度 \([\text{m}]\)

    • \(L_{z}\) : 垂直域頂部高度 \([\text{m}]\)

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} (\mathbf{L}\tilde{f}, \tilde{g}) &\overset{\text{定義 2}}{=}& \frac{1}{L_x L_y L_z} \int_{0}^{L_z} \int_{0}^{L_y} \int_{0}^{L_x} \left( (L\tilde{f})_1 g_1^* + (L\tilde{f})_2 g_2^* + (L\tilde{f})_3 g_3^* + \frac{1}{c^2} (L\tilde{f})_4 g_4^* + \frac{g^2}{N^2} (L\tilde{f})_5 g_5^* \right) dxdydz \\ &\overset{\text{推導 1(a)}}{=}& \frac{1}{L_x L_y L_z} \int_{0}^{L_z} \int_{0}^{L_y} \int_{0}^{L_x} \left( \frac{\partial f_4}{\partial x} g_1^* + \frac{\partial f_4}{\partial y} g_2^* + \left(\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma\right)f_4 + g f_5 \right) g_3^* + \frac{1}{c^2} c^2 \left[ \frac{\partial f_1}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial f_2}{\partial y} + \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} - \Gamma f_3 g_4^* - g f_3 g_5^* \right) dxdydz \\ &\overset{\text{推導 2(a)(b)(c)(d)(e)(f)}}{=}& \frac{1}{L_x L_y L_z} \int_{0}^{L_z} \int_{0}^{L_y} \int_{0}^{L_x} \left( -\frac{\partial g_1^*}{\partial x}f_4 - \frac{\partial g_2^*}{\partial y}f_4 - \frac{\partial g_3^*}{\partial z}f_4 + \Gamma f_4 g_3^* + g f_5 g_3^* - \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial x}f_1 - \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial y}f_2 - \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial z}f_3 - \Gamma f_3 g_4^* - g f_3 g_5^*\right) dxdydz \\ &=& - \frac{1}{L_x L_y L_z} \int_{0}^{L_z} \int_{0}^{L_y} \int_{0}^{L_x} \left( \frac{\partial g_1^*}{\partial x}f_4 + \frac{\partial g_2^*}{\partial y}f_4 + \frac{\partial g_3^*}{\partial z}f_4 - \Gamma f_4 g_3^* - g f_5 g_3^* + \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial x}f_1 + \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial y}f_2 + \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial z}f_3 + \Gamma f_3 g_4^* + g f_3 g_5^*\right) dxdydz \\ &=& -\frac{1}{L_x L_y L_z} \int_{0}^{L_z} \int_{0}^{L_y} \int_{0}^{L_x} \left( \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial x}f_1 + \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial y}f_2 + \left( \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial z} + \Gamma g_4^* + g g_5^* \right) f_3 + \left( \frac{\partial g_1^*}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial g_2^*}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial g_3^*}{\partial z} - \Gamma g_3^* \right) f_4 - g g_3^* f_5 \right) dxdydz \\ &=& - \frac{1}{L_x L_y L_z} \int_{0}^{L_z} \int_{0}^{L_y} \int_{0}^{L_x} \left( \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial x}f_1 + \frac{\partial g_4^*}{\partial y}f_2 + \left( \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} + \Gamma\right)g_4^* + g g_5^* \right) f_3 + \left[ \frac{\partial g_1^*}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial g_2^*}{\partial y} + \left(\frac{\partial}{\partial z} - \Gamma\right)g_3^* \right] f_4 + \frac{g^2}{N^2} (-\frac{N^2}{g} g_3^*) f_5 \right) dxdydz \\ &\overset{\text{定義 2}}{=}& -(\tilde{f}, \mathbf{L}\tilde{g}) \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

證明完畢 ！算子 \(\mathbf{L}\) 在此內積定義下確實是反厄米特（Skew-Hermitian）算子。

Question 1 : Density of standing waves in cavity#

Consider the radiation inside a cavity of absolute temperature \(T\) whose walls are perfect reflectors to be a series of standing em waves(電磁波). This is a three-dimensional generalization of standing waves in a stretched string. The condition for standing waves in such a cavity is that the path length from wall to wall, whatever the direction, must be a whole number of half-wavelengths, so that a node occurs at each reflecting surface. Proof that the number of independent standing waves \(G(\nu)d\nu\) in the frequency interval between \(\nu\) and \(\nu+d\nu\) per unit volume in the cavity is

\[G(\nu)d\nu = \frac{8\pi\nu^2}{c^3} d\nu \]

Question 1 - Answer#

邏輯主軸: 邊界條件限制了波長 \(\to\) 波長離散化形成網格點 \(\to\) 計算球體體積求總數 \(\to\) 轉成頻率求微分 \(\to\) 乘以2（偏振）得到密度。

\[G(\nu)d\nu = \frac{8\pi\nu^2}{c^3} d\nu\]

  • \(\nu\) (頻率) \([\text{Hz}] = [\text{s}^{-1}]\)

  • \(d\nu\) (頻率區間)：一個極小的頻率範圍（從 \(\nu\) 到 \(\nu + d\nu\)）\([\text{Hz}] = [\text{s}^{-1}]\)

  • \(c\) (光速)：真空中光速，\(2.99 \times 10^8 [\text{m} \cdot\text{s}^{-1}]\)

  • \(G(\nu)\) (駐波密度 / 狀態密度，Density of States)：在單位體積內、每單位頻率區間中，可以容納多少個駐波模式

  • \(G(\nu)d\nu\) (總駐波數密度)：代表在頻率介於 \(\nu\) 與 \(\nu + d\nu\) 之間，單位體積內共有多少個駐波模式 \([\text{m}^{-3}]\)

假設與已知 (Assumptions & Preliminaries)#

  • 【已知 1】 考慮3D正方體空腔情況：

    • 三維正方體:一個邊長為 \(L\)、體積為 \(V = L^3\)

    • 模式方向有3個 \(x\)、\(y\)、\(z\)

    • \(k_x = n_x \frac{\pi}{L}, \quad k_y = n_y \frac{\pi}{L}, \quad k_z = n_z \frac{\pi}{L}\)

    • \(n_x, n_y, n_z\) 都是正整數 (\(1, 2, 3, \dots\)) (\(n_x, n_y, n_z \in \mathbb{N}\))

  • 【已知 2】 波向量的總長度平方 \(k^2\) 為：

    \[k^2 = k_x^2 + k_y^2 + k_z^2 = \left(\frac{\pi}{L}\right)^2 (n_x^2 + n_y^2 + n_z^2)\]

  • 【定義 1】 模式空間中的半徑 \(R\) ：

    \[R^2 \overset{\text{let}}{=} n_x^2 + n_y^2 + n_z^2\]

    • 「\(n\) 空間」的三維網格系統裡。每一個座標點 \((n_x, n_y, n_z)\) 都代表一個合法的「駐波模式」， 如何計算特定半徑範圍內，總共有多少個點？

  • 【已知 3】 波向量 \(k\) 轉換透過真空中電磁波的角頻率 \(\omega\) ：

    \[k = \frac{2\pi\nu}{c}\]

    • \(\omega = c k\)

    • \(\omega = 2\pi\nu\)

  • 【已知 4】 大模式空間的半徑\(R\)內的整數點近似體積 ：

    \[\text{半徑R內的整數點} = \frac{4}{3}\pi R^3\]

    • \(R = \frac{2L\nu}{c} \sim 3.33 \cdot 10^5\)

      • \(L = 0.1 \text{ m}\)

      • \(\nu = 5 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}\)

      • \(c = 3 \times 10^8 \text{ m}\cdot \text{ s}^{-1} \)

    • \(\frac{\text{表面誤差的方塊數}}{\text{總體積的方塊數}} \propto \frac{R^2}{R^3} = \frac{1}{R} \approx \frac{1}{333,333} \approx 0.0003\%\)

  • 【定義 2】 模式空間的半徑在 \(R\) 以內的總模式數 \(N(R)\)：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} N(R) &=& \text{半徑R內的合法整數點} \\ &=& \left(\text{合法整數點比例}\right) \times \left(\text{半徑R內的整數點}\right) \\ &\overset{\text{已知 4}}{\sim}&\left(\text{合法整數點比例}\right) \times \frac{4}{3}\pi R^3 \\ &\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& \frac{1}{8} \times \frac{4}{3}\pi R^3 \\ &=& \frac{\pi}{6} R^3 \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 5】 模式空間的半徑 \(R\) 對頻率轉換 ：

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} R &=& \sqrt{\left(\frac{L}{\pi}\right)^2 k^2}\\ &=&\frac{L}{\pi} k \\ &=& \frac{L}{\pi} \left( \frac{2\pi\nu}{c} \right) \\ &=& \frac{2L\nu}{c}\\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

    • 【定義 1】\(R^2 \overset{\text{let}}{=} n_x^2 + n_y^2 + n_z^2\)

    • 【已知 2】\(k^2 = \left(\frac{\pi}{L}\right)^2 (n_x^2 + n_y^2 + n_z^2)\)

    • 【已知 3】\(k = \frac{2\pi\nu}{c}\)

  • 【已知 6】 電磁波的極化 ：

    \[dN_{\text{total}} = 2 dN\]

    • 於電磁波而言，給定一個行進方向（一個波向量 \(\mathbf{k}\)），它可以有兩種互相垂直的獨立偏振方向（例如 \(x\) 偏振與 \(y\) 偏振的橫波）。因此，實際的駐波數量必須乘以 2

  • 【定義 3】 單位體積內的駐波密度 \(G(\nu)\) ：

\[G(\nu)d\nu = \frac{dN_{\text{total}}}{V}\]

證明#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} N(\nu) &\overset{\text{定義 2}}{=}& \frac{\pi}{6} R^3 \\ N(\nu)&\overset{\text{已知 5}}{=}& \frac{\pi}{6} \left( \frac{2L\nu}{c} \right)^3 \\ N(\nu)&=& \frac{\pi}{6} \frac{8 L^3 \nu^3}{c^3} \\ N(\nu)&=& \frac{4\pi L^3}{3 c^3} \nu^3 \\ N(\nu)&\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& \frac{4\pi V}{3 c^3} \nu^3 \\ \frac{dN}{d\nu} d\nu&=& \frac{d}{d\nu} \left[ \frac{4\pi V}{3 c^3} \nu^3 \right] d\nu \\ dN&=& \frac{4\pi V}{c^3} \nu^2 d\nu\\ dN_{\text{total}} &\overset{\text{已知 6}}{=}&2\frac{4\pi V}{c^3} \nu^2 d\nu \\ \frac{dN_{\text{total}}}{V} &=&\frac{8\pi }{c^3} \nu^2 d\nu \\ G(\nu) d\nu &\overset{\text{定義 3}}{=}&\frac{8\pi }{c^3} \nu^2 d\nu \\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

Question 2 Average energy per standing wave#

Suppose the oscillators in the cavity walls do not have a continuous distribution of possible energies but have only the specific energies

\[\epsilon_{n}=nh\nu\]

Proof that the average energy per oscillator in the cavity walls—and so per standing wave—is

\[\bar{\epsilon} = \frac{h\nu}{e^{h\nu/kT}-1}\]

(Hint: Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution function \(f_{MB} (\epsilon)=Ae^{-\epsilon/kT} \) )

Question 2 - Answer#

已知與假設#

  • 【已知 1】無窮等比級數求和:

    \[\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} x^n = 1 + x + x^2 + \dots = \frac{1}{1-x}\]

    • \(|x| < 1\) ，本題中 \(x = e^{-h\nu/kT}\) ，符合條件

  • 【已知 2】等式:

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} x^n &\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& \frac{1}{1-x} \\ x \frac{d}{dx} \left[ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} x^n \right] &=& x \frac{d}{dx} \left[ \frac{1}{1-x} \right] \\ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} n x^n &=& \frac{x}{(1-x)^2} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

  • 【已知 3】能量量子化:

    \[\epsilon_{n}=nh\nu\]

  • 【已知 4】Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution function:

    \[f_{MB}(\epsilon)=Ae^{-\epsilon/kT}\]

  • 【定義 1】亞變數:

    \[x = e^{-h\nu/kT}\]

proof#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \bar{\epsilon} &=& \frac{\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \epsilon_n f_{MB}(\epsilon_n)}{\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} f_{MB}(\epsilon_n)}\\ &\overset{\text{已知 3,4}}{=}&\frac{\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (nh\nu) A e^{-nh\nu/kT}}{\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} A e^{-nh\nu/kT}}\\ &\overset{\text{定義 1}}{=}& \frac{h\nu \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} n x^n}{\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} x^n}\\ &\overset{\text{已知 2}}{=}& \frac{h\nu \frac{x}{(1-x)^2}}{\frac{1}{1-x}}\\ &=& h\nu \frac{x}{1-x}\\ &\overset{\text{定義 1}}{=}&h\nu \frac{e^{-h\nu/kT}}{1 - e^{-h\nu/kT}}\\ &=& \frac{h\nu \cdot e^{-h\nu/kT} \cdot e^{h\nu/kT}}{(1 - e^{-h\nu/kT}) \cdot e^{h\nu/kT}}\\ &=& \frac{h\nu}{e^{h\nu/kT} - 1} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

Question 3#

Express the Planck radiation formula in terms of wavelength.

Question 3 - Answer#

已知與假設#

  • 【已知 1】頻率 \(\nu\) 與波長 \(\lambda\) 的關係式:

    \[\nu = \frac{c}{\lambda}\]
    \[d\nu =-\frac{c}{\lambda^2} d\lambda\]

    • 能量是正的 \(|d\nu| = \frac{c}{\lambda^2} |d\lambda|\)

  • 【已知 2】Jacobiany 在 \(u(\nu)\) 與 \(u(\lambda)\) 的關係式:

    \[|u(\nu)d\nu| = |u(\lambda)d\lambda|\]

proof#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} u(\nu)d\nu &=& G(\nu) \bar{\epsilon} d\nu \\ u(\nu)d\nu&\overset{\text{Q 1,2}}{=}& \left( \frac{8\pi \nu^2}{c^3} \right) \left( \frac{h\nu}{e^{h\nu/kT} - 1} \right) d\nu \\ u(\nu)d\nu&=& \frac{8\pi h \nu^3}{c^3 (e^{h\nu/kT} - 1)} d\nu \\ u(\lambda)d\lambda &\overset{\text{已知 1,2}}{=}& \frac{8\pi h \left( \frac{c}{\lambda} \right)^3}{c^3 \left( e^{\frac{h(c/\lambda)}{kT}} - 1 \right)} \left( \frac{c}{\lambda^2} d\lambda \right)\\ u(\lambda)d\lambda &=& \frac{8\pi hc}{\lambda^5} \frac{1}{e^{hc/\lambda kT} - 1} d\lambda \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

Question 4#

A photon of frequency 𝜈 is scattered by an electron initially at rest. Verify that the maximum kinetic energy of the recoil electron is

\[KE_{max}=\frac{2h^{2}\nu^{2}/mc^{2}}{1+2h\nu/mc^{2}}\]

Question 4 - Answer#

已知與假設#

  • 【已知 1】康普頓波長位移公式（Compton shift formula）:

    \[\Delta \lambda = \lambda' - \lambda = \frac{h}{mc}(1 - \cos\theta)\]

    • 能量守恆、動量守恆

    • 讓電子獲得最大動能 \(\theta = 180^\circ\)

  • 【已知 2】散射後頻率 \(\nu'\) :

    \[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \lambda' - \lambda &=& \frac{h}{mc}(1 - (-1)) \\ \lambda' - \lambda &=& \frac{2h}{mc}\\ \frac{c}{\nu'} - \frac{c}{\nu} &=& \frac{2h}{mc}\\ \frac{1}{\nu'} - \frac{1}{\nu} &=& \frac{2h}{mc^2}\\ \frac{1}{\nu'} &=& \frac{1}{\nu} + \frac{2h}{mc^2} \\ \frac{1}{\nu'} &=& \frac{mc^2 + 2h\nu}{\nu mc^2}\\ \nu' &=& \frac{\nu mc^2}{mc^2 + 2h\nu}\\ \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

proof#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} E_{\text{光子,初}} + E_{\text{電子,初}} &\overset{\text{能量守恆}}{=}& E_{\text{光子,末}} + E_{\text{電子,末}}\\ h\nu + mc^2 &=& h\nu' + (KE_{max} + mc^2)\\ KE_{max} &=& h\nu - h\nu' \\ KE_{max}&\overset{\text{已知 2}}{=}& h\nu - h\left( \frac{\nu mc^2}{mc^2 + 2h\nu} \right) \\ KE_{max}&=& h\nu \left( 1 - \frac{mc^2}{mc^2 + 2h\nu} \right)\\ KE_{max}&=& h\nu \left( \frac{mc^2 + 2h\nu - mc^2}{mc^2 + 2h\nu} \right) \\ KE_{max}&=& h\nu \left( \frac{2h\nu}{mc^2 + 2h\nu} \right) \\ KE_{max}&=& \frac{2h^2\nu^2}{mc^2 + 2h\nu}\\ KE_{max} &=& \frac{2h^2\nu^2 / mc^2}{1 + 2h\nu / mc^2} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

Question 5#

The gravitational potential energy \(U\) relative to infinity of a body of mass m at a distance \(R\) from the center of a body of mass \(M\) is \(U = -GmM/R\).

(a) If \(R\) is the radius of the body of mass \(M\) , find the escape speed \(v_e\) of the body, which is the minimum speed needed to leave it permanently.

(b) Obtain a formula for the Schwarzschild radius of the body by setting \(v_e=c\), the speed of light, and solving for \(R\). (Of course, a relativistic calculation is correct here, but it is interesting to see what a classical calculation produces.)

Question 5 - Answer#

已知與假設#

  • 【已知 1】力學能守恆定律（Conservation of Mechanical Energy）:

    \[E_{total} = K + U = \text{常數}\]

    • 初始總能量：\(E_i = \frac{1}{2}mv_e^2 - \frac{GmM}{R}\)

      • 初始動能：\(K_i = \frac{1}{2}mv_e^2\)

      • 初始位能：\(U_i = -\frac{GmM}{R}\)

    • 最終總能量：\(E_f = 0\)

      • 最終動能：\(K_f = 0\)（因為停下來了）

      • 最終位能：\(U_f = -\frac{GmM}{\infty} = 0\)

proof (a) 尋找逃逸速度 \(v_e\)#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} \frac{1}{2}mv_e^2 - \frac{GmM}{R} &\overset{\text{已知 1}}{=}& 0 \\ \frac{1}{2}mv_e^2 &=& \frac{GmM}{R}\\ v_e^2 &=& \frac{2GM}{R}\\ v_e &=& \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R}} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]

proof (b)推導史瓦西半徑 \(R_S\)#

\[\begin{split}\begin{gather*} v_e &\overset{\text{Q5.(a)}}{=}& \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R}} \\ c &=& \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R_s}}\\ R_s &=& \frac{2GM}{c^2} \end{gather*}\end{split}\]
